# YieldLoop NFT Program
A Two-Tier Membership System for Supporters & Founders

The YieldLoop NFT Collection is a utility-based membership system designed to help fund the development of the YieldLoop platform and the broader Veridic Finance framework. These NFTs do not represent equity, do not provide governance rights, and do not offer profit-sharing. They unlock utilities and privileges within the platform and community.

This collection consists of two membership levels:

- **Supporter NFTs ($300)**
- **Founder NFTs ($1000)**

Both tiers contribute to building a verified-finance ecosystem, but Founders receive enhanced access and participation benefits.

## 4. Compliance & Safety Notes

The YieldLoop NFT system is purposely structured to avoid any resemblance to securities, investment contracts, or equity instruments.

### These NFTs:
- Do **not** grant ownership in YieldLoop  
- Do **not** provide rights to revenue, profits, or dividends  
- Do **not** offer governance or voting power  
- Do **not** create expectations of ROI  
- Are **sold as digital memberships** with platform-related utility  

The Founder Engagement Incentive is:
- A **marketing budget allocation**  
- **Not correlated to YieldLoop financial performance**  
- **Participation-based and discretionary**  
- Similar to loyalty rewards, ambassador rewards, or community bounties  

This framework ensures compliance with U.S. and international standards for digital asset utility programs.
